I'm Eric, and I am a Fullstack Web Developer, Graphic Designer, Teacher, and Musician in the Evansville, Indiana area.

I have worked as an Administrative Assistant and Lecturer at a university, Director of Customer Success at a tech startup, High School Computer Science Teacher, Church Music Director, Freelance Graphic Designer, Coding Bootcamp Facilitator, and - most recently - a Senior Customer Support Analyst with an edtech company. My versatile skillset and diverse experiences allow me to think outside the box and identify innovative solutions to big problems.

As a teacher, I had this running joke with my students that I always ask them what they want to be when they grow up because I'm still taking suggestions. I am a lifelong learner, and learning is my hobby and my superpower. I am especially interested in finding ways to connect my love of the arts, technology, and cognition. My ultimate career goal is to become a full-time web developer and eventually build my own tech startup.

In my spare time, I enjoy playing piano, reading, cooking, traveling, woodworking, and spending time with my family.
